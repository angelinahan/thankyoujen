Part II: The End(ish)

01. Media Arts Capstone

I'm so glad I was able to join your capstone class in my last semester. It feels like a full-circle moment that my time as a CS and Design major here is bookended by 5D and Media Arts Capstone.

02. Ideation

I had the beginnings of the idea for A Day in Your Clothes a year or two ago, but I think developing it in this environment made it even stronger. The project was initially so exploratory and broad; it was helpful to hear that being loose and exploratory wasn't a bad thing.

03. Concept and Practice

Reflecting on my current practice helped me see themes in my own work that I never saw before. I've had difficulty summarizing my work in the past because I like using many different mediums. It was so helpful to sit down and think about my influences and past work.

04. Applying to exhibitions

Talking about this process was such helpful practical knowledge. I really appreciate everything you shared, and I also really enjoyed seeing your work (it's so cool)! After this lecture, I ended up submitting a piece to a magazine and being accepted! I think I'm going to participate in a Zoom event they're having in a few weeks, so that will be the first time I talk about my work in public! Baby steps are happening -- thanks for making it all seem possible and attainable.

05. Installation

Installing my final project was so exciting. I'm glad you mentioned the goal was to show it in Gallery360 early in the semester -- knowing that it would be a physical, in-person experience definitely influenced the form and allowed me to consider the design of the installation.
